ESPN Fantasy Football Insider

User Persona

  • Name: Joe Kittle
  • Age: 38
  • Gender: M
  • Profession:NFL Insider for ESPN/Fantasy Football Expert
  • Technology:Iphone11,Macbook Pro
  • Attributes:
    • Comfortable with filming video and uploading to ESPN Website.
    • Proficient at breaking down NFL game film and evaluating player talent.
    • Able to record daily fantasy football podcast.
  • Needs:
    • Needs to be able to announce when new podcasts and content have been posted on ESPN Fantasy website.
    • Needs to be able to interact with fans and followers
    • Needs to have daily schedule laid out in advance

User Story:

As a host and content provider for ESPN. Joe would like to add content to his followers daily.

Usecase/Interaction Flow:

Title: Uploading a podcast/video

Description:Joe wants to upload his video to ESPN server, and share with fantasy football followers.

Name of User and their role: Joe Kittle

Pre-conditions: Must have ESPN account and logged in

Post-conditions: Video is posted to NFL Insider account, followers are notified.

Interaction Flow:

  • Joe logs into ESPN website account.
  • Joe selects video/podcast he wants to upload to ESPN
  • Joe presses upload button from his cpu
  • ESPN server uploads video file to ESPN web page
  • Joe inputs title, description, date about video and selects "finished"
  • ESPN server sends notification to all Joe's followers of new content.

Frequency of Use: Multiple times a week
